# Product Overview

This is a digital voting system designed for Swedish associations (föreningar), specifically developed for Mensa Sverige's annual meetings. The system replaces paper-based postal voting with a secure, transparent, and user-friendly digital solution.

## Core Purpose

Enable democratic decision-making at association meetings through:

- Pre-meeting voting (förtidsröstning)
- Live voting during meetings
- Remote participation via streaming
- Transparent vote counting and verification

## Key Principles

- **Open Source**: AGPLv3 or MIT license for maximum transparency and reusability
- **Security First**: Cryptographic separation between voter identity and vote content
- **Accessibility**: Works on all devices (mobile-first design)
- **Portability**: Runs anywhere from laptop to cloud cluster
- **Simplicity**: Designed for small associations (< 100 members) but scales to thousands

## Target Users

- **Primary**: Swedish associations conducting annual meetings
- **Personas**: Meeting chair, secretary, members, election committee, auditors
- **Scale**: Optimized for small associations but supports up to 10,000+ members

## Compliance Requirements

- Swedish association bylaws (stadgar)
- GDPR with archive law exceptions
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility
- Swedish and English language support
